Authorial Models — are the result of observation, practical analysis, and the systematic documentation of recurring patterns.
They are not abstract concepts or theoretical constructions.
They are practice-derived outcomesof decisions and actions — applicable in real life.
These models were formed over years —
through working with individuals,
with teams,
с with managerial challenges,
with crises and growth points.
Each model represents:
a structured understanding of a situation
a logical framework for decision-making
consideration of consequences
• the ability to adapt to context
They do not require belief.
They are not meant to be accepted as Truth.
Their purpose is to be tested.
These models are designed for those who want not merely to react to events, but to understand what is happening and consciously influence the direction of movement.
That is why they became part of the book «Practicularium» — as documented experience shaped into practical and applicable tools.
