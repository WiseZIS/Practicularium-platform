Welcome! I am Ivan Zaitsev.
Today, I hold a Master of Business Administration (MBA General), I am a certified Project Manager, a Master of Metallurgy, and PRO level AI Content Maker (AI Creator).
In the past, I have also been:
  • a poet and author of the book “The Story of One Man’s Feelings”
  • an assistant to a city council deputy
  • the head of a working group at a regional state administration
  • co-founder and technical director of a manufacturing and trading company
  • founder of a consulting office
  • a freelancer with private practice
I have more than 20 years of practical experience working with real people — teams, large working groups, management processes, decisions, responsibility, and consequences.
For over 15 years, I have held managerial positions in business, the public sector, and consulting.
My journey began early — at the age of 17, — when I entered university and moved to an unknown me city.
That was when I first felt the freedom of choice — dangerous, risky, sometimes destructive, yet deeply formative.
It was this path that gave me immense practical experience and awakened a deep interest in knowledge — not academic, but living and conscious.
I have always needed to understand things personally: why something works, how it is structured, and what happens if you apply it as practic in real life.
Over time, this drive turned learning into something close to an addiction.
But most importantly, I always documented the results — my own, my colleagues’, and the ideas, described in books — both successful and flawed.
